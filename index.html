This is a demonstration of the leaflet web map library to create a thematic map using a custom tile set from Mapbox

Smith River Study Areas and Streams

I wanted to experiment with a "dark mode" inspired map elements theme. I have chosen a dark background color for the page, as well as styled my popups, legend, and basemap layer selector to map for a cohesive theme.

I wanted the colors for the geojson layers to be bright enough to separate from the custom mapbox tileset (which had colors chosen from a photo of my study site), but I find they turned out a little too bright and I'm not entirely thrilled with them.

I also tried styling the native leaflet zoom buttons, but couldn't get it to work :(
